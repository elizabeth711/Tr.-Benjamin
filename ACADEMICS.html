Curriculum Overview

At KALIRO HIGH SCHOOL, we follow the Uganda National Curriculum, providing a robust educational foundation from Senior One to Senior Six. Our curriculum is designed not only to prepare students for national examinations but also to equip them with critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills essential for higher education and future careers. We emphasize a balanced approach, combining theoretical knowledge with practical application.

Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) Admission Requirements

For admission into Senior One (S.1) at Kaliro High School, candidates are assessed based on their performance in the Primary Leaving Examination (PLE), administered by the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB). Understanding the PLE grading system and divisions is crucial.

PLE Subjects & Grading System

The PLE assesses students in four core subjects, each graded from Distinction 1 (D1) to Failure 9 (F9):

Grade Description
**D1**Distinction 1 (Excellent)
**D2**Distinction 2 (Very Good)
**C3**Credit 3 (Good)
**C4**Credit 4 (Above Average)
**C5**Credit 5 (Average)
**C6**Credit 6 (Pass, Adequate)
**P7**Pass 7 (Marginal Pass, Basic)
**P8**Pass 8 (Barely Passed, Minimal)
**F9**Failure 9 (Failed, Below Standard)

The four core PLE subjects are:

  • **English Language**
  • **Mathematics**
  • **Integrated Science**
  • **Social Studies (SST)**

PLE Divisions Explained

A candidate's overall performance in the PLE is categorized into divisions based on their aggregate score from the four subjects. A lower aggregate indicates better performance.

Division Description
**Division 1**Excellent overall performance.
**Division 2**Very good overall performance.
**Division 3**Good overall performance.
**Division 4**Fair pass, average to basic performance.
**Division 7 (Ungraded/X)**Failed in one or more core subjects, not meeting promotion standards.
**Division U (Ungraded/F)**Overall failure in the examination, minimal performance.

For further details on PLE criteria and examinations, please refer to the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) official website.
